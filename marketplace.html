Timeframe

Role

UX | UI Designer

Tools

Figma | Illustrator | After Effects

Problem

Smile CDR wants to re-design the consumer-faced side of Marketplace to appeal to their audience and create a sense of trust and security on the platform. They wish to accomplish this and have it developed before initial demo reel.

Research

User flows created to understand information architecture of Marketplace and to onboard myself.

Review current Marketplace environment to determine where areas of improvement are required.

Key insights from research analysis:

    •   A new color system is desired in the re-design of Marketplace
    •   Seperate the landing page and browsing of products
    •   Prioritize user accessibility to provide user-friendliness and key information

Proposal

How can we re-design Marketplace that is modern and user-friendly?

Design Process

Marketplace is a platform dedicated to providing users with products and services aimed to help improve their health and experience.

Visitor Experience from Product Board to determine what features and pages would be valuable for users based on the research and ideation.

Design workshop to determine what features and pages would be valuable for users based on the research and ideation.

Breadboard Exercise designed to make sure the usability and available features were accessible and straightforward.
