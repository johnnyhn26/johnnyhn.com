Marketplace

Designed to customize your health and experience.

Timeframe

7 Months | Jul - Jan '22

Role

UX | UI Designer

Tools

Figma | Illustrator | After Effects

Problem

Smile CDR wants to re-design the consumer-faced side of Marketplace to appeal to their audience and create a sense of trust and security on the platform. They wish to accomplish this and have it developed before initial demo reel.

Research

Onboarded by creating user flows to understand the information architecture of Marketplace and familiarize.

Conducted a FigJam brand strategy workshop to determine Marketplace's identity. Collaborated with stakeholders, PMs, business analysts, and solution architects to break down Marketplace into key insight components.

Conducted user research for buyer and seller personas in association with PM and Senior Experience Architects to determine what features would attract early adopters and create validation for Marketplace.

Key insights from research analysis and workshop:

    •   A new color system is desired in the re-design of Marketplace
    •   Seperate the landing page and browsing of products
    •   Prioritize user accessibility to create user-friendliness in discovering health products

Proposal

How can we re-design Marketplace to have a fresh new look, retaining core user functionality in discovering health products on the platform?

Design Process

The Smile CDR Marketplace is a platform dedicated to helping users discover, try, and buy health products that fufill their needs.

Breadboarding exercise designed to quickly diagram what the landing page will look like roughly on web and mobile.

Ideations

Multiple re-iterations were done to finalize the concepts of Marketplace.
