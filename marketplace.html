Marketplace

Designed to customize your health and experience.

Timeframe

7 Months | Jul - Jan '22

Role

UX Designer

Tools

Figma | Illustrator | After Effects

Problem

Smile CDR wants to create a consumer-faced platform that can connect buyers and sellers for health services and products.

Research

Onboarded by creating user flows to understand and familiarize the information architecture of Marketplace.

Conducted a FigJam brand strategy workshop to determine brand identity in collaboration with stakeholders, product managers, business analysts, and solution architects.

Created user research questions for buyer and seller personas with product managers and senior info architects to determine what features would attract early adopters and create validation.

Key insights from research analysis:

    •   Easy access for buyers to discover and read up on health products
    •   Remove communication barriers between buyer and seller
    •   Focus on user accessibility to create a seamless experience

Proposal

How can Marketplace be a leading platform in showcasing health products and services while creating a seamless transactional experience for buyers and sellers?

Design Process

The Smile CDR Marketplace is a platform that showcases health products and services, allowing buyers and sellers to connect, discover and fufill their needs.

Breadboarding exercise designed to quickly diagram what the landing page will look like roughly on web and mobile.

Ideations

Multiple re-iterations were done to finalize the concepts of Marketplace.
