Jabuka Games

A simple and fun word game that stands out amongst its competitors.

Timeframe

4 Months | Aug - Sep '19

Role

Design | Marketing

Tools

Premiere Pro | Photoshop | Illustrator

Problem

Jabuka Games Inc. was in its early stages as a boardgame company and needed to establish itself as a prominent player within the industry. Their word game "Jabuka" had a lot of potential to be popular amongst families and casual gamers alike. They just needed the push through marketing and branding to stand out within the saturated market.

Proposal

How could this game be branded to compete with existing competitors? What would the brand identity look like that would make it distinguishable?

Solution

The online and social spread of Jabuka was increased through a combination of thoughtful graphic design and user-focused marketing. A website was developed that contained appealing graphics, promotional videos and access to online checkout and delivery. The game's appeal to kids was leveraged with social media presence on all major platforms in the form of fun videos and designs.

Design Process

Graphic Design

Fun and bright graphics were made to appeal to Jabuka's younger target audience using Illustrator and Photoshop.

Videos

Image link to a popular promotional video that was designed and animated using Premiere Pro.

Animations

Animated using Photoshop and Illustrator as part of marketing campaign for all major social platforms.
